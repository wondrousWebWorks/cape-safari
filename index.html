About

Established in 1954, Cape Safari has been offering a world-class safari experience for every budget and taste. Located in the majestic Western Cape region of South Africa, we enjoy a much sought-after Mediterranean climate and unparalleled natural beauty.

Multiple Awards, including Most Memorable Safari Experience.

Experienced guides will ensure you don't miss a thing and get as close to the action as possible.

An all-encompassing variety of fauna and flora unique to the Cape region.
